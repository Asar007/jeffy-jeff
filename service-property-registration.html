Property Registration & Mutation

Property transactions in India involve complex registrations, mutations, and title verifications. Our legal team navigates the sub-registrar offices, municipal authorities, and revenue departments on your behalf.

What's Included

Complete Property Paperwork

From sale deeds to mutation entries, every document handled with legal precision.

Sale Deed Registration

Complete registration of sale/purchase agreements at sub-registrar office.

  • Deed drafting and legal review
  • Stamp duty calculation and payment
  • Sub-registrar appointment and filing

Property Mutation

Updating ownership records in municipal/revenue records after transfer.

  • Mutation application at municipal office
  • Revenue record update (7/12 extract, Khata)
  • Name transfer in property tax records

Title Verification

Thorough title search to ensure clean ownership before any transaction.

  • 30-year title search at sub-registrar office
  • Encumbrance certificate procurement
  • Legal opinion on title clearance

Certificate Procurement

Getting essential property documents from government offices.

  • Encumbrance Certificate (EC)
  • Khata/Patta certificate
  • RERA registration verification
