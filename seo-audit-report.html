Local SEO: 18 / 100 — the single weakest area. This is a local service business with no published NAP, no Google Business Profile signals, and no city landing pages. Local fixes are the highest-leverage area on the entire site.

Critical Issues — Fix Immediately

CRITICAL 1Zero NAP data anywhere on the site

No phone, address, or email is present in the footer, contact page, or schema. The footer states only "Registered in India" with no CIN, GST number, or legal name. For a local service business handling YMYL categories (elder care + legal/financial), this is fatal — it blocks GBP verification, citations, trust signals, and schema validity.

Fix: Publish phone + registered office address in the footer, the contact page, and the ProfessionalService schema today. Even a service-area business benefits from a disclosed city of registration.

CRITICAL 2Placeholder WhatsApp number on quote-builder.html

Line 466 of quote-builder.html contains https://wa.me/91XXXXXXXXXX — a literal placeholder. Any user who taps "WhatsApp Us" gets a broken link. On a YMYL service site, a broken contact path is a hard trust failure.

Fix: Replace 91XXXXXXXXXX with the real business WhatsApp number before any further marketing activity.

CRITICAL 3Unverifiable testimonials and unsourced statistics

Six 5-star testimonials use generic Name / Job / City with initials-only avatars and no platform links. Homepage statistics (62%, 78%, 53%, "₹3.2 L average loss", "4 in 10 vehicles") have no source citations. Under the September 2025 Google Quality Rater Guidelines, specific-looking numbers without traceable sources read as fabricated authority, particularly on YMYL pages.

Fix: Link each testimonial to a public review source (GBP, Trustpilot). Source every statistic to a named report (ANAROCK NRI survey, RBI, etc.) — or rewrite them as softer experiential claims ("NRIs we work with consistently report…").

CRITICAL 4No author / founder / expert attribution on YMYL pages

The Emergency Response and legal service pages promise 5-minute ambulance dispatch, Power of Attorney execution, tax filing, and court monitoring with no named professional behind any of it. The footer "About Us" link is href="#" and no about.html exists in the repository. The September 2025 QRG explicitly requires YMYL content to demonstrate who is accountable.

Fix: Build about.html with named founders, photos, and credentials. Add a visible "Service led by [Name], licensed advocate/CA/doctor, registration number] block on each YMYL service page.

CRITICAL 5signup.html and quote-builder.html are indexable

Both appear in sitemap.xml (lines 36–37) and neither carries a noindex,nofollow meta tag. Google will index a bare signup wall and a placeholder-laden quote form — neither has organic search value.

Fix: Add <meta name="robots" content="noindex,nofollow"> to both pages, add Disallow: entries in robots.txt, and remove both URLs from sitemap.xml.

CRITICAL 6POA page is structurally unrankable for its target query

The page is ~425 words. The dominant SERP format for "power of attorney NRI India" is informational guides of 1,500–3,000 words with step-by-step process, country-specific (UK/USA/UAE) apostille-vs-notarisation guidance, and FAQ schema. The current page leads with pricing on an information-dominant query — a structural mismatch Google will not feature.

Fix: Expand to 1,500+ words. Lead with process content. Move pricing below the fold. Add FAQPage schema with at least 6 questions.

High-Priority Issues

HIGH 7Render-blocking Google Fonts on every page

Line 21 of every page is a synchronous <link rel="stylesheet"> to Google Fonts. This blocks the first paint until the browser fetches the font CSS from fonts.googleapis.com. Estimated impact: +300–800ms LCP penalty for users in Asia, Middle East, and the Americas.

Fix: Replace with the preload + media="print" onload="this.media='all'" non-blocking pattern across all 45 pages. Highest-ROI performance change available.

HIGH 8LCP element is an unoptimized PNG with no dimensions

The hero logo NRI-BRIDGE-INDIA_LOGO-WHITE-PNG.png is the LCP candidate on every page. It is PNG (no WebP/AVIF), has no width/height attributes, no fetchpriority="high", and no <link rel="preload">. The JS-injected nav loads the same image at 80px with no dimensions, causing CLS.

Fix: Convert to WebP (50–70% size reduction). Add explicit width/height, fetchpriority="high", and a preload hint in <head>. Set dimensions on the nav logo inside nav.js.

HIGH 9Schema is under-specified across the site

The homepage ProfessionalService node lacks telephone, address, geo, city-level areaServed, aggregateRating, and sameAs. No BreadcrumbList JSON-LD on any service page despite visible HTML breadcrumbs. contact.html and quote-builder.html have no schema at all.

Fix: Deploy enriched LocalBusiness/ProfessionalService schema with three city-level areaServed entries (Chennai, Coimbatore, Kanyakumari) and full NAP. Add BreadcrumbList JSON-LD on all 26 service pages. Add AggregateRating to homepage once real reviews exist.

HIGH 10Plan-tier naming inconsistency

service-rent-collection.html references Bronze / Silver / Platinum tiers; the homepage pricing section uses Starter / Standard / Premium. This is a visible contradiction for any user navigating between pages.

Fix: Standardise tier names sitewide to Starter / Standard / Premium.

HIGH 11Service pages are thin for YMYL category

service-rent-collection.html ~280–300 words. service-emergency-response.html ~260 words. Both handle financial and elder-care medical topics respectively. The rent collection page does not cover NRO/NRE account tax implications, FEMA, or TDS obligations on rental income. The emergency page does not list networked hospitals, caretaker ratios, or response-team qualifications.

Fix: Expand each service page to at least 900 words. Add a "What to expect" narrative, a real scenario walkthrough, and a 5-question FAQ.

HIGH 12Three Google Business Profiles are missing

The business operates in Chennai, Coimbatore, and Kanyakumari. There are zero GBP signals on the site (no Maps embed, no Place ID, no review widget, no GBP badge). For a 3-city operation, three separate GBP listings are required.

Fix: Create and verify three GBP listings with city-specific primary categories ("Property Management Company" or "Home Health Care Service" as appropriate). Wrong category is the #1 negative local ranking factor.
