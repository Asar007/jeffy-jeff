Family Care

Companion & Caretaker

Loneliness and lack of daily assistance are the biggest challenges for elderly parents of NRIs. Our vetted companions provide emotional support, daily help, and trained caregiving — becoming your trusted presence on the ground.

What's Included

Compassionate, Professional Care

More than just help — genuine companionship and emotional support for your parents.

Regular Companionship Visits

Trained companions who visit your parents regularly for social interaction.

  • Conversation, walks, and social engagement
  • Hobby and activity participation
  • Emotional well-being monitoring

Daily Assistance

Help with everyday tasks that become difficult with age.

  • Grocery shopping and errand running
  • Bill payments and bank visits
  • Household coordination (maids, cooks)

Trained Caretakers

For parents needing more intensive support, we provide trained caregivers.

  • Certified elderly care professionals
  • Mobility assistance and fall prevention
  • Medication administration and monitoring

Regular Reporting to You

Detailed updates so you always know how your parents are doing.

  • Photo and video updates from visits
  • Weekly mood and activity reports
  • Concern escalation with recommendations
