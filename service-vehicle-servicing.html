Vehicle Care

Periodic Servicing & Maintenance

A vehicle left unservice deteriorates fast — engine oil sludges, batteries die, tyres crack. We coordinate regular servicing at authorised centres to keep your car in peak condition, even when parked for months.

What's Included

Complete Servicing Coordination

From scheduled maintenance to emergency repairs, your vehicle stays road-ready.

Scheduled Servicing

Bi-annual or quarterly servicing based on manufacturer recommendations.

  • Oil & filter changes at authorised centres
  • Brake pad inspection and replacement
  • Air conditioning servicing and gas refill

Preventive Maintenance

Proactive checks to catch problems before they become expensive repairs.

  • Battery health monitoring and replacement
  • Tyre pressure, tread depth, and rotation
  • Coolant, transmission, and brake fluid checks

Service Records & Reports

Complete digital service history maintained for resale value and warranty claims.

  • Photo + video documentation of each service
  • Digital service booklet maintained
  • Cost breakdown and comparison reports

Idle Vehicle Care

Vehicles parked for extended periods need special attention to prevent deterioration.

  • Bi-weekly engine start and idling
  • Battery trickle charging
  • Tyre rotation to prevent flat spots
